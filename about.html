Why Choose Us?

Best Shopping Store In The Country

Can you imagine shopping without easy return and exchange? Well, we can’t either. Besides the ease of shopping from the comfort of your home and the availability a large number of styles and products, what makes online shopping tick for millions of Cara customers is the hassle-free exchange option. However, some customers take undue advantage of this service and attempt to abuse it. They take this service for granted and start returning their products more frequently without realising how their decision is going to adversely affect their customer score. When customers opt to return a product, the system records their returns rate and if the returns rate is higher than average, then customers are charged convenience fees for their next orders and the score also impacts their Cara Insider (our loyalty program) benefits. In order to enjoy free shipping and our loyalty program benefits, it’s important to keep the number of returns within an optimal range so that we know that this feature we have extended for genuine customer needs is not being misused. While cara provides a hassle-free returns experience with benefits such as wide 30 day return window and doorstep refund post successful pickup, a high ratio product returns would add layers of unsatisfactory shopping experience on Myntra. A customer with high returns is charged a fee on all orders placed regardless of order value. If you are looking for an effortless and smooth shopping experience and to enjoy all the customer benefits on cara, then you know the hack- shop responsibly and keep a check on your returns!
